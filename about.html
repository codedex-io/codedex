About

# Codédex


Codédex is the brand new way to learn to code for kids and adults alike. Journey through the fantasy land of Python, HTML/CSS, or JavaScript, earn exp. points (XP) to unlock new regions and items, and collect all the badges at your own pace.

This is currently the Beta version, so let us know what you think! (づ｡◕‿‿◕｡)づ:･ﾟ✧



# The Team


The folks behind Codédex have taught Computer Science at Columbia, NYU, CUNY, and have brought programming to over 50 million users around the world at Codecademy.

Each exercise is curated by world-class educators and developers and works both in classrooms and online setting.



# Pricing


The best part? Codédex maps and projects are totally free!

If that's not enough, we also have Codédex++ for $12.99 a month. It's an inclusive community with weekly events, guest speakers, code mentors, peer reviews, office hours, and awesome fellow learners who will join you on your coding journey.

Every dollar we earn will go back into the product & commuity to make them better. You don't want to miss this!
